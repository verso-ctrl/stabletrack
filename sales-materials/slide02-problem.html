The Problem

📋 Scattered Records

Health records in binders, spreadsheets, text messages. When's the coggins due?

📅 Scheduling Chaos

Whiteboard calendars, sticky notes. Did someone tell the vet about Tuesday?

💸 Billing Headaches

End of month means hours creating invoices. Who paid? Who didn't?

👥 Communication Gaps

Did the caretaker get the vet's instructions? Does the owner know what happened?

📱 No Mobile Access

Everything's on the office computer. Can't check anything from the barn.

🔄 Staff Turnover

When someone leaves, their knowledge goes with them.
